Leadership, Strategic Planning, and Execution Training

Certification by BlossomWorks Company Limited, Kenya

1. Course Objectives

This training aims to equip participants with the knowledge and skills required for effective leadership, strategic planning, and execution within organizational settings. By the end of the course, participants will:

  • Understand the core principles of leadership and strategic management.
  • Develop skills for formulating and executing strategic plans.
  • Enhance decision-making and problem-solving capabilities.
  • Learn techniques for effective stakeholder engagement and collaboration.
  • Improve their ability to monitor and evaluate strategic initiatives.

2. Course Content (Topics)

Module 1: Fundamentals of Leadership

  • Leadership theories and styles
  • Ethical leadership and governance
  • Transformational vs. transactional leadership
  • Leadership in crisis management

Module 2: Strategic Planning

  • Understanding the strategic planning process
  • Setting vision, mission, and organizational objectives
  • Environmental scanning (PESTEL and SWOT analysis)
  • Stakeholder analysis and engagement

Module 3: Strategy Formulation and Implementation

  • Developing strategic goals and action plans
  • Resource allocation and risk management
  • Aligning strategy with organizational culture
  • Communication and change management

Module 4: Strategy Execution and Performance Management

  • Tools and frameworks for effective execution (Balanced Scorecard, OKRs)
  • Monitoring and evaluation of strategic plans
  • Key performance indicators (KPIs) and impact measurement
  • Adapting to emerging trends and challenges

Module 5: Leadership in Public and Private Sectors

  • Leadership and strategic planning in NGOs
  • Leadership within government structures (National and County levels)
  • Strategy execution in private sector organizations
  • Public-private partnerships for strategic success

3. Target Group

This course is designed for professionals in leadership and managerial roles, including:

  • NGO Officials (Program Managers, Directors, and Team Leaders)
  • National and County Government Officials (Department Heads, Policy Makers, and Administrators)
  • Private Sector Leaders (CEOs, Business Executives, Entrepreneurs, and Strategy Officers)

4. Expected Outcomes

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will:

  • Demonstrate enhanced leadership competencies.
  • Effectively develop and execute strategic plans.
  • Improve organizational decision-making and resource management.
  • Strengthen stakeholder engagement and partnership-building.
  • Implement monitoring and evaluation techniques for continuous improvement.

5. Duration

  • Full Course Duration: 5 Days (40 Hours)
  • Delivery Mode: In-person training, Virtual sessions (if applicable)
  • Training Methods: Lectures, Case Studies, Group Discussions, Role Plays, and Practical Exercises

6. Training Evaluation

Evaluation will be conducted through:

  • Pre- and post-training assessments to measure knowledge gained.
  • Participant feedback surveys to assess the effectiveness of training delivery.
  • Case study analysis and presentations to gauge application of learning.
  • Final assessment and certification upon successful completion.

Certification: Participants who complete the training and meet evaluation criteria will receive a Certificate of Completion from BlossomWorks Company Limited, Kenya.

Module 3: Data Collection and Analysis

  • Qualitative and quantitative data collection methods
  • Developing data collection tools (surveys, interviews, focus groups)

Module 4: Reporting, Learning, and Decision-Making

  • Writing effective M&E reports for stakeholders and donors
  • Communicating results through dashboards and presentations
  • Adaptive management and continuous learning
  • Case studies on best practices in project evaluations

Module 5: Impact Assessment and Sustainability

  • Measuring long-term impact and sustainability
  • Participatory M&E approaches
  • Engaging communities and stakeholders in evaluation processes
  • Strategies for integrating M&E into project cycles

3. Target Group

This course is designed for professionals involved in project monitoring and evaluation, including:

  • NGO Officials (M&E Officers, Program Managers, and Project Coordinators)
  • National and County Government Officials (Planning Officers, Development Officers, and Policy Analysts)
  • Private Sector Leaders (CSR Managers, Business Development Officers, and Consultants)

4. Expected Outcomes

Upon successful completion of the course, participants will:

  • Design and implement effective M&E frameworks.
  • Develop and use appropriate data collection and analysis tools.
  • Improve project reporting and decision-making processes.
  • Enhance accountability and transparency in project management.
  • Apply M&E best practices to drive project impact and sustainability.
